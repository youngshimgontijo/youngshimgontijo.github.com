Hello! My name is Youngshim Gontijo, I am currently a senior illustration major at Massachusetts College of Art and Design. My work tends to be light-handed, where I work delicately with a decorative style. I have always been drawn to Aboriginal art, so much of my work reflects how I love the graphic, flat, line work. I focus a lot on nature as well as human and animal interactions. youngshim.gontijo@gmail.com Located in Boston, MA
