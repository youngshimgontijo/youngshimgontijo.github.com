About/Contact

Hello! My name is Youngshim Gontijo, a recent graduate from Massachusetts College of Art and Design as an illustration major. I work sensitively, and at times, meticulously. I have always been drawn to Aboriginal art that much of my work reflects the graphic, flat, line work. I focus a lot on nature and on the concept of light vs. dark. I have more recently been working on fitting my style of illustration to meet the needs of potential branding quality. I want to get into logo design and branding in the future, combining elements of my illustrations with type. Located in Boston, MA
