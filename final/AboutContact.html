About/Contact

Hello and welcome! My name is Youngshim Gontijo. I am a graduate with a Bachelor of Fine Arts in Illustration from Massachusetts College of Art and Design, located in Boston, MA. I work sensitively and at times meticulously. A lot of my work reflects Aboriginal Art, which I'm drawn to; this contains a lot of flat, graphic line work. I also like to focus my art on nature and on the concept of light versus dark. I want to further pursue logo design and branding by combining elements of my illustrations with type. Please contact me via email in regards to buying art. I am also available for freelance work. Enjoy!
