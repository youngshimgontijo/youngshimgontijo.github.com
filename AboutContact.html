About/Contact

Hello and welcome! My name is Youngshim Gontijo. I am a graduate with a Bachelor of Fine Arts in Illustration from Massachusetts College of Art and Design, located in Boston, MA. My art focuses on nature and on the concept of light versus dark. I am pursuing other interests in logo design and branding by combining elements of my illustrations with type. Please contact me via email in regards to buying art. I am also available for freelance work. Enjoy!
